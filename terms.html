1. Academic Integrity Policy

GAIME Start Learning is committed to Ethical Generative AI Use. You are here to learn how to use generative AI tools to enhance your thinking, not to replace it.

What We Will Do

  • Use generative AI to brainstorm topics, outline arguments, and critique your logic
  • Turn your messy notes into organised study guides using NotebookLM
  • Show you how to use generative AI as a research assistant and thinking partner
  • Teach you to fact-check generative AI outputs and use tools safely
  • Help you understand concepts faster through generative AI explanations
  • Build systems that help you work smarter, not cheat

What We Will Not Do

  • Generate full essays, assignments, or exams to submit as your own work
  • Help you cheat or violate your institution's academic policies
  • Solve take-home exams or complete graded assignments
  • Do your work for you instead of teaching you the skills
  • Help you bypass academic integrity systems

Termination for Violations: If during a session I determine you are attempting to use generative AI in violation of academic integrity policies, I reserve the right to stop the session immediately (no refund), terminate our coaching relationship (remaining package sessions forfeited), and decline future services.

2. Scope of Services

GAIME Start Learning is a generative AI coaching service focused on helping individuals build real, practical generative AI fluency. Primary focus is Google Workspace generative AI — Gemini, NotebookLM, and AI features across Google Workspace. General prompt engineering principles covered in sessions apply across all major AI tools, so if you use ChatGPT, Claude, or other platforms, foundational concepts transfer. Technical support for non-Google platforms is outside scope.

What We Do

Educational Coaching: Explore tools like Gemini and NotebookLM to improve personal productivity, learning, and creative workflows.

Hands-On Learning: Live demonstrations with your actual content, screen-sharing guidance while you practice, strategic discussions about what's possible, and custom examples relevant to your goals.

Creative Applications: Turn documents into podcasts or study guides, analyse videos without watching them fully, brainstorm ideas and overcome creative blocks, use generative AI for roleplay scenarios and problem-solving.

What We Don't Do

Technical Support: Fixing Google account settings, troubleshooting software, resolving sync issues, or IT helpdesk services.

Software Development: Code review, debugging, building apps, or ongoing technical maintenance.

Professional Services: Legal advice, financial planning, medical guidance, or subject matter expertise outside of generative AI tools.
