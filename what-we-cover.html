What We Cover

Every session is custom-built for you. Here's what's possible — I'll recommend the right mix based on your specific challenge.

Three tracks Learning & Research Productivity Creative

Learning & Research

For students, researchers, and lifelong learners

You can learn to:

  • Turn dense PDFs into audio podcasts for easier studying
  • Analyze YouTube lectures without watching them in full
  • Create study guides from messy notes
  • Use AI ethically without academic integrity issues
  • Fact-check AI outputs and spot hallucinations

Work & Productivity

For professionals, freelancers, and teams

You can learn to:

  • Draft emails faster with Gemini Side Panel
  • Turn meeting recordings into action items
  • Build repeatable workflows that save hours each week
  • Use AI to manage projects and tasks
  • Generate reports and summaries quickly

Creative & Advanced

For content creators, writers, and power users

You can learn to:

  • Transform content formats: PDF to podcast, video to summary
  • Brainstorm ideas when you're stuck
  • Use AI for roleplay and simulation
  • Discover advanced Gemini and NotebookLM features
  • Build creative workflows tailored to your process
Important Boundaries

What's In and Out of Scope

What We Do

  • Creative strategy and productivity coaching
  • Hands-on learning with your actual content
  • Teaching prompting and AI workflows
  • Ethical AI use guidance
  • Building systems to help you work smarter

What We Don't Do

  • Technical support (fixing Google accounts, troubleshooting)
  • Software development (code review, debugging, building apps)
  • Ghostwriting (generating essays to submit as your own)
  • Academic integrity violations
  • Professional advice: legal, financial, or medical

Academic Integrity Promise

We teach you how to enhance your thinking, not replace it. AI is a study tool and thinking partner, not a shortcut to bypass learning. If you're looking for someone to write essays for you, this isn't the right service.
