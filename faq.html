01Common Questions

FAQ

Everything you need to know before booking. Still have a question? Just ask in your email.

Do I need technical skills?

+

No. If you can use Google Docs and email, you're qualified. I teach non-technical people how to use AI tools practically and confidently.

Will this work with ChatGPT too?

+

Yes. Prompting techniques apply across all AI tools. My focus is Google's ecosystem: Gemini and NotebookLM. But the skills transfer.

What if I can't attend my session?

+

Let me know at least 24 hours before your session and we'll reschedule at no charge. Less than 24 hours' notice, or a no-show without contact, means the session is forfeited.

Do you offer refunds?

+

No. All payments are final. Sessions are live and your time slot is reserved specifically for you. If you're unsure whether this service is right for you, ask questions before booking, I'm happy to chat.

Is this ethical for students?

+

Yes. I teach you how to use AI to enhance your learning, not to cheat. We don't generate essays for you. We teach you to use AI as a study tool and thinking partner, in line with your institution's policies.

Virtual or in-person?

+

Both. Virtual sessions run over Google Meet and are open to anyone in Canada. In-person sessions are available at public venues (coffee shops) in North York, between Finch and Sheppard. I provide location options.

How many sessions will I need?

+

Most clients feel confident after 2 to 3 sessions. We assess together after your first session, no pressure to commit to more upfront.

Do I need the paid version of Gemini?

+

Start with Gemini Free if you want to try it first. We recommend AI Plus for most sessions. AI Pro is more than enough if you've already got it. Ultra is only worth it for the extra storage and features, that's your call. Plans and pricing: gemini.google/subscriptions.

What should I bring to the session?

+

Your laptop, your actual project or content, and a willingness to learn by doing. I'll guide you through everything else. A phone or tablet alone isn't sufficient for hands-on work.

Can my parent or employer book on my behalf?

+

Yes. Just include in the email who the session is for and their situation. I'll tailor the intake form and session plan accordingly.

Still Have Questions?

Just ask in your email. No question is too basic, I'll answer everything before we book.
