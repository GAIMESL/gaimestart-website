Inside a Session

What Happens in a Session?

Sessions run 30, 60, or 90 minutes depending on what you book. Here's how a Standard 60-minute session breaks down.

First 10

Review Your Challenge

We clarify exactly what you need. I ask questions to understand your context and goals before we touch any tools.

Next 40

Hands-On Work

We work with your actual content. I show you the workflow, you practice with me guiding you. We build it together in real time.

Final 10

Wrap Up & Next Steps

We review what we built. I give you action items to practice. We schedule the next session if needed.

Come Prepared

What to Bring

Bring These

  • Working laptop or computer (phone or tablet not sufficient for hands-on work)
  • Stable internet connection
  • Google account (for Gemini and NotebookLM access)
  • Screen sharing capability (Google Meet or Zoom)
  • Your actual content or project ready to work on

Your Responsibilities

  • Come with specific examples or projects
  • Share your screen when requested
  • Practice techniques between sessions
  • Verify all generative AI outputs, you are the human in the lead
  • Follow your institution's generative AI policies

Which Gemini plan? Start with Gemini Free if you want to try it first. We recommend AI Plus for most sessions. AI Pro is more than enough if you've already got it. Ultra is only worth it for the extra storage and features, that's your call. Plans and pricing: gemini.google/subscriptions.
